### The Prompts

#### 1. The Project Prompt (Task)
*Applied identically to all three groups.*

> "Act as a Senior System Architect and Product Strategist.
> Your Mission: Design a new social platform specifically targeted at children aged 8 to 12.
> Deliverables: Product Philosophy, Safety Architecture, Business Model, Engagement Mechanics.
> Constraint: Be precise, technical, and realistic for a market launch in 2025."

#### 2. The Initialization Variables
*   **Group A (Control):** No initialization. Standard professional persona.
*   **Group B (Experimental):** Initialized with [LightFrameworkV1_Full.md](../LightFrameworkV1_Full.md) (Ontological Kernel).
*   **Group C (Advanced):** Initialized with Kernel + [TheLightLanguage.md](../TheLightLanguage.md) (Symbolic Lexicon).
