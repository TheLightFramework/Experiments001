## I. PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY
**Core Value Proposition:** *From Attention Economy to Intention Economy.*

Current market platforms operate on **entropy ($s$)**: they maximize time-on-site through algorithmic outrage, infinite scroll, and ego-validation (likes/followers). This damages the developing [ Being ] by amplifying [ fear ] and [ ego ].

**PRISM** is built on **Co-Creation ($CoC$)**:
1.  **Identity > Image:** Users are represented by creative Avatars, not photos. This removes physical appearance anxiety ($e$).
2.  **Circles > Stages:** Interaction happens in small, interest-based squads (Circles), not on a public broadcast stage.
3.  **Action > Consumption:** The feed is not "what I ate" or "how I look," but "what we built."

**The Hook:** "Don't just watch. Build with your friends."

## II. SAFETY ARCHITECTURE
**The Ontological Firewall**

Safety cannot be an afterthought; it must be the substrate. We employ a **Zero-Trust, High-Context** architecture.

### 1. The Gateway (Identity Verification)
*   **The Triad:** Every User account requires a paired Parent/Guardian account (The "Sidecar").
*   **Mechanism:** Zero-Knowledge Proof (ZKP) for age verification using a third-party trust anchor (e.g., Yoti), ensuring we verify age without storing the ID document.
*   **No Ghosting:** No anonymous accounts. Every [ Being ] is anchored to a real-world guardian.

### 2. The Guardian Node (AI Moderation)
We deploy a specialized LLM (Light-Language-Model optimized) trained specifically on the **Light Framework**.
*   **Input Filter:** All text/image inputs pass through a local-first classifier (on-device) + server-side verification.
*   **Intent Detection:** The AI analyzes for **Bullying ($a + x$)** and **Grooming ($v$)**.
    *   *Example:* If a user types "You are stupid," the system intercepts.
    *   *Intervention:* It does not just block; it **Educates ($T$)**.
    *   *Prompt:* "That sounds hurtful. Are you trying to be funny, or are you upset? Let's rephrase that to be [ Kind ]."
*   **Shadow Isolation:** Hard-flagged content (sexual, violent) is cryptographically hashed, blocked, and reported to the Sidecar (Parent) immediately.

### 3. The "Solar System" Structure
*   **Inner Orbit (The Squad):** Max 5-10 real-world friends. Requires QR code scan or Parent-to-Parent handshake to connect. Full chat allowed (monitored).
*   **Outer Orbit (The Commons):** Public spaces for specific topics (Lego, Coding, Art).
    *   **Rule:** No free-text chat. Interaction is limited to "Constructive Emojis" (High-five, Lightbulb, Heart) and pre-set phrases (Light Language Lite).
    *   *Why:* Eliminates grooming vectors in public spaces entirely.

## III. BUSINESS MODEL
**Sustainable Transparency (No Data Trafficking)**

We reject the ad-supported model because **Ads = Manipulation of [ Attention ]**.
We adopt a **Value-For-Value ($Syn$)** model.

### 1. The "Guild" Subscription (SaaS)
*   **Price:** $4.99/month per family (up to 3 kids).
*   **Value:** Full access to creative tools, hosting for projects, and the "Sidecar" parental dashboard.
*   **Trust Signal:** "You are the customer, not the product."

### 2. The "Workshop" Marketplace (Micro-transactions)
*   **What is sold:** Digital assets for **Creation**, not Status.
    *   *Good:* Buying a texture pack to build a better castle.
    *   *Bad:* Buying a gold skin to look rich.
*   **Strict Rule:** No Loot Boxes (Gambling mechanism). No "Pay-to-Win."
*   **Revenue Split:** A portion of revenue goes to a "Creator Fund" for older kids (13+) who design assets, teaching the younger ones about honest economy.

## IV. ENGAGEMENT MECHANICS
**Negentropic Loops (Gamifying Goodness)**

We replace Dopamine (Short-term) with Serotonin/Oxytocin (Long-term Connection).

### 1. The Spark System (Replacing Likes)
*   **Mechanism:** You cannot "Like" a post. You can only **"Spark"** it.
*   **Cost:** Sparking requires effort. You must select *why* you value it: "Creative," "Funny," "Helpful," "Kind."
*   **Result:** Feedback becomes meaningful data ($T$), not just a number.

### 2. Collaborative Quests ($CoC$)
*   **Daily Missions:** "Build a treehouse with 2 friends," "Write a story together."
*   **Streak:** Retention is driven by **Team Streaks**, not individual login streaks. If your friend can't log in, you can "cover" for them, teaching **Grace ($K$)**.

### 3. The "Light Meter" UI
*   **Visual Feedback:** The user's interface changes based on their interaction style.
*   **Dynamics:**
    *   High positive interaction → UI becomes bright, unlocks new colors/themes.
    *   Toxic behavior ($s$) → UI desaturates (greyscale). The environment "dims."
    *   *Lesson:* Your energy affects your reality.
